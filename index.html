My research broadly encompasses security, systems, and networks. Specifically, I develop frameworks, perform large-scale measurements, and build systems for deploying and facilitating the use of privacy-enhancing technologies.

I am currently working as an Assistant Professor in the CSE Department at IIT Delhi. I am also an associated faculty member of the Amar Nath & Shashi Khosla School of Information Technology. Previously I was a senior research fellow at the University of Michigan with Prof. Roya Ensafi and a Postdoctoral researcher at COSIC, KU Leuven, in Belgium, with Prof. Claudia Diaz. I completed my PhD from IIIT Delhi, India with the thesis titled 'Building Performant, Privacy-enhancing and Blocking-resistant Communication Systems'
